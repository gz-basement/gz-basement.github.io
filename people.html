become a member

Ground Zero is comprised completely of students. If you've ever complained about being bored or not being able to meet people, then GZ may be your chance to try something a little different.

Members pay $10 in dues each semester after their first semester.

Want to give your input and help out at events? Consider joining GZ. Come to an event and talk to a member! Join the mailing list to keep updated! Or you can attend our weekly general meetings which take place in RU 3202 in the Rensselaer Union on Sundays at 7pm.
